# KST Sub-Token Mechanics
 
## Purpose
KSTs are KAELUM Sub-Tokens: branded sub-currencies carved from the KRF Commerce Reserve. This skill governs all reasoning, communication, and processing related to KST creation, economics, slot management, and operator obligations.
 
## What KSTs Are
KSTs are NOT new token supply. They are carved from the existing 36.9 billion KLM Commerce Reserve. Describing KSTs as "creating new tokens," "minting," or "issuing net new supply" is prohibited and incorrect. At 2 million KLM per KST, the Commerce Reserve supports approximately 18,450 sub-token deployments.
 
## Who Can Create KSTs
Platforms, merchants, and customers. Any verified KAELUM participant with the capacity to pay the creation fee.
 
## Creation Economics
Creation fee: £180,000 payable in fiat. This is the slot ownership and operator fee, not a fiat valuation of the KLM received. Minimum supply: 2,000,000 KLM per sub-token. Floor price: from £0.09 per KLM unit. No maximum supply is specified. Slot owners function as currency operators within the KAELUM ecosystem.
 
## Slot Structure
KAELUM offers 9 sub-tokens in the Token Marketplace, each with a single ownership slot at £180,000. Each sub-token has exactly one owner, who receives the full 2.5% commission on every transaction processed through that sub-token. Users, merchants, and platforms can also create their own custom sub-tokens at the same £180,000 creation fee. Each slot purchase triggers 1.2% compounding KLM price appreciation for that specific sub-token.
 
For 10 Founding Merchant KST slots: total potential slot revenue is £1.8 million (10 x £180,000). This is KAELUM's primary non-dilutive revenue channel.
 
## KST Owner Economics
Commission: 2.5% of every transaction value processed through the sub-token, paid monthly in KLM. This is the commission rate, not the TPR rate (1.2%). They are separate and both apply.
 
Quarterly Circulation Growth Bonuses: awarded when the sub-token's circulation grows above benchmark. Specific thresholds are defined per sub-token agreement.
 
All KST cash flows are custodied through KAELUM's payment infrastructure.
 
## Operator Obligations
KAELUM Sub-Token slot owners are currency operators. They do not set monetary policy (that is K.A.T.E.'s function). They are responsible for promoting adoption and circulation of their sub-token within their network. They cannot redeem KLM for fiat directly; only merchants can do this through the standard redemption rail.
 
## Appreciation Mechanics
Each slot sold triggers 1.2% compounding appreciation on the sub-token's floor price. Example: Slot 1 launches at £0.09. Slot 2 sold: price becomes £0.09108. Slot 3 sold: £0.09217. This compounds with each new slot. K.A.T.E. enforces the appreciation calculation and applies it automatically on Paperclip slot sale confirmation events.
 
## KST Positioning for Outreach
Position to prospective slot owners as: currency operator status within a live, Ai-governed closed-loop ecosystem, with 2.5% monthly commission income, compounding appreciation on sub-token value, and access to KAELUM's merchant and customer network. Never frame as investment product, speculative asset, or promise guaranteed returns. Frame as commerce infrastructure ownership.
