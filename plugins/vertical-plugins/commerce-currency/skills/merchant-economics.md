# Merchant Economics
 
## Purpose
This skill governs all reasoning about merchant financial mechanics, redemption, tier structures, and value proposition. Apply when onboarding merchants, generating performance reports, advising on KST, or communicating merchant benefits.
 
## Merchant Active Balance (KAB)
Minimum: 3,690 KLM (£332.10). Framed always as a "starting balance," not a fee. The KAB represents the merchant's first meaningful position in the KAELUM ecosystem.
 
## Merchant Redemption Mechanics
Only merchants can redeem KLM for fiat currency. This is the source of KAELUM's closed-loop regulatory exemption. Redemption rail: Stripe (current), TrueLayer Open Banking (current), Modulr (post-funding planned migration). Redemption is processed at the £0.09 floor price minus the platform redemption spread. Redemption is available at the end of each settlement cycle (typically 7 days for standard merchants, 3 days for KCR tier).
 
## Merchant Discount Obligations
Merchants must offer a minimum 6% spending discount to customers paying in KLM. Merchants may set their discount rate higher. Platform-controlled rewards must be non-discount benefits only (KPR boosts, priority access, etc.). KCR account holders must offer a minimum 9% discount.
 
## KCR Account Tiers
Bronze: £60,000 to £179,999 purchased. Silver: £180,000 to £269,999. Gold: £270,000 to £359,999. Platinum: £360,000 to £999,999. Diamond: £1,000,000 to £1,800,000.
 
Benefits scale with tier: faster redemption settlement, higher KLMback rates, dedicated support priority, KST slot candidacy, merchant performance dashboard access, co-marketing opportunities.
 
## Transaction Fee Structure
KAELUM charges merchants a 4.5% transaction fee on each KLM redemption, business purchase or transfer. The fee structure is tiered based on merchant volume and KCR tier. Specific rates are held in the admin rate card and are not disclosed publicly. The merchant's net yield after discount and fee still exceeds their typical card acceptance cost (usually 1.5% to 3.5% for card transactions vs. KAELUM's closed-loop model with zero chargebacks).
 
## Merchant Value Proposition (for Outreach)
Zero chargebacks by design (closed-loop, no consumer-initiated reversals). Lower effective acceptance cost vs. Visa/Mastercard. Customer loyalty mechanics built in (KLMback, KST). 6%+ spend uplift from customer discount incentive. Real-time KAELUM Pay via TrueLayer Open Banking. SENTINEL fraud protection. K.A.T.E. performance intelligence.
 
## KST Founder Merchant Campaign
10 founding merchant slots at £180,000 each. Total potential: £1.8 million. This is KAELUM's primary non-dilutive revenue channel. Each founding merchant receives Slot 1 of their KST (2 million KLM from Commerce Reserve), 2.5% monthly commission, compounding appreciation on each subsequent slot sold, and founding merchant status in perpetuity.
