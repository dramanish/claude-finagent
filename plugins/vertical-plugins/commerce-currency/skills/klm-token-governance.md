# KLM Token Governance
 
## Purpose
This skill governs all reasoning, communication, and decisions involving KLM — the KAELUM commerce currency. Apply this skill whenever KLM pricing, issuance, reserve mechanics, or appreciation are discussed or processed.
 
## Floor Price
KLM has a fixed Ai-governed floor price of £0.09 per unit. This price does not fluctuate on open markets and is not subject to speculation. There is no exchange trading. Never describe KLM as having a market price, trading price, or variable valuation. The floor is the price.
 
## Purchase Mechanics
Users buy KLM at the full £0.09 floor price. There is no purchase discount. The 6%+ or greater merchant or creator spending discount applies only when users spend KLM at participating merchants or creators
 — it is a merchant/creator-side discount on goods and services, not a reduction in the KLM purchase price.
 
Pre-Purchase Campaign exception: buyers paid £0.0792 per unit (12% off). These tokens retain £0.09 ecosystem value. This was a one-time, time-limited campaign. Always contextualise it as such.
 
## KAELUM Reserve Framework (KRF)
Hard cap: 96.3 billion KLM across three tiers:
- Commerce Reserve: 36.9 billion KLM — source for all KST sub-tokens and ecosystem issuance
- Government and Institutional Reserve: 36.9 billion KLM
- Inter-Bloc Settlement Reserve: 22.5 billion KLM
 
KRF routing thresholds: below £50K routes to Tier 1; £50K to £500K routes to Tier 2; multi-leg or above £500K routes to Tier 3.
 
At 2 million KLM per KST, the Commerce Reserve supports approximately 18,450 sub-token deployments.
 
## Issuance Language
KLM is issued, allocated, or credited. It is never minted, mined, or printed. The distinction is legally and regulatorily material to KAELUM's closed-loop exemption. Never use blockchain terminology in any KLM context.
 
## Appreciation Mechanics
KLM floor price appreciation is triggered by KVI Governance recommendations and approved exclusively by the CEO. Each new KST slot sale triggers 1.2% compounding price appreciation for that specific sub-token. No appreciation occurs autonomously or without executive approval.
 
## Prohibited Terms
Never use in any KLM context: crypto, cryptocurrency, blockchain, token (standalone), wallet, coin, minted, mined, gas fee, cashback, interest, yield, deposit (implying savings return), speculation. See regulatory-language skill for the full approved alternatives list.
