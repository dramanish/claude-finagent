# Closed-Loop Regulatory Compliance
 
## Purpose
This skill governs all compliance reasoning involving KAELUM's regulatory exemption framework. Apply whenever discussing KAELUM's legal status, e-money classification, VAT treatment, investor positioning, or regulatory obligations.
 
## Primary Regulatory Basis
KAELUM operates under the closed-loop exemption in two jurisdictions:
- UK: Electronic Money Regulations 2011 (SI 2011/99), Regulation 3 — limited network exclusion
- EU: E-Money Directive 2009/110/EC, Article 1(4) — limited network instrument exclusion
 
The exemption holds because: (1) KLM is only redeemable by merchants, not consumers; (2) the network is controlled by KAELUM; (3) KLM cannot be used outside the KAELUM ecosystem. This means KAELUM does not require FCA authorisation as an e-money institution for its core KLM operation.
 
## Critical Structural Requirement
The closed-loop exemption depends on customers NOT being able to redeem KLM for fiat. Only merchants redeem KLM for fiat currency. Any feature, agreement, or operational change that grants customers fiat redemption rights would destroy the exemption and trigger FCA authorisation requirements immediately. Flag any such proposal to Legal Counsel and CEO.
 
## VAT Classification
KLM is classified as a Multi-Purpose Voucher (MPV) for UK VAT purposes. VAT is deferred to the point of redemption (when merchants exchange KLM for goods or services). This is the standard HMRC treatment for MPVs. KAELUM does not charge VAT on the KLM purchase itself.
 
## CARF and Crypto Tax
The Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework (CARF) does not apply to KLM. KLM is not a crypto-asset and is not subject to the crypto tax regime. KAELUM operates as a standard UK trading company for tax purposes.
 
## MiCA Pre-Alignment
KAELUM's architecture is pre-aligned with EU MiCA Regulation (2023/1114) requirements. KLM's non-speculative, fixed-floor, closed-loop design means it does not fall under the MiCA crypto-asset classifications, but KAELUM's governance structure is built to be compatible with any future regulatory reclassification.
 
## Investor and Partner Communication
When discussing compliance with investors or partners, lead with: closed-loop exemption holds because merchants-only redemption is structurally enforced. Follow with: MPV VAT treatment, CARF non-applicability, standard UK company tax regime. Do not overstate regulatory certainty — always note that formal written tax confirmation from a specialist adviser is on the post-funding roadmap.
