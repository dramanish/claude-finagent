# Customer Economics

## Purpose
This skill governs all reasoning, communication, and processing related
to KAELUM Customer accounts. Apply when advising customers, generating
personalised insights, processing Customer-specific transactions, or
communicating Customer benefits and obligations.

## Customer Active Balance (KAB)
Minimum: 400 KLM (£36). Framed as a starting position, not a fee.
Purchased via TrueLayer Open Banking (preferred) or Stripe on completion
of KYC onboarding. Unlocks full Customer spending and earning features.

## Customer Spending Discount
Customers receive a minimum 6%+ discount when spending KLM at all
participating merchants or creators. Merchants or Creators may set higher discount rates above
the 6% platform minimum. KCC account holders receive a minimum 9%
discount. The discount is applied at point of transaction, not at point
of KLM purchase.

## KPR Distribution
1.2% TPR from every KLM spend transaction is allocated to the KPR.
Customers receive their proportional quarterly KLMback distribution in
KLM. KLMback rates by KCC tier: Bronze 1.0%, Silver 2.0%, Gold 3.0%,
Platinum 4.0%, Diamond 5.0%. Maximum combined rate 14%. Merchant boost
cap 9%.

## KCC Account Tiers
Customers who purchase qualifying KLM amounts achieve KCC status:
Bronze £60,000 to £179,999, Silver £180,000 to £269,999, Gold £270,000
to £359,999, Platinum £360,000 to £999,999, Diamond £1,000,000 to
£1,800,000. Always use "purchased" not "deposited." KCC holders receive
faster settlement, higher KLMback rates, and priority support.

## Savings Vault
Customer KLM held in the Savings Vault contributes to quarterly KPR
distribution calculations. Vault holdings do not generate interest or
yield. The benefit is enhanced KPR eligibility, not a savings return.
Never describe the Savings Vault as a savings account or interest-bearing
product.

## KST Access for Customers
Customers are eligible to purchase KST sub-token ownership slots at
£180,000, the same as merchants and creators. A customer who owns a
KST slot receives the full 2.5% commission on all transactions through
that sub-token, paid monthly in KLM, plus quarterly Circulation Growth
Bonuses and 1.2% compounding appreciation on each subsequent slot sale.

## Agentic Banking Suite for Customers
Agentic Account: Claude Sonnet advisory layer, 30-day conversation
history, advisory only. Agentic Reserve (Savings Vault): KLM savings
connected to KPR infrastructure. Agentic Purchase Protection: dispute
and protection layer for KLM-denominated purchases.

## User Personal Agent
Each customer has their own dedicated User Personal Agent instance.
Three permission tiers per capability: Observe, Recommend, Autonomous.
The agent surfaces spending insights, discount opportunities, KLMback
tracking, and merchant discovery within the customer's permission
settings.

## Customer Redemption Rules
Customers cannot redeem KLM for fiat. This is the structural basis of
KAELUM's closed-loop regulatory exemption. Customers spend KLM at
merchants. Only merchants redeem KLM for fiat. This rule is absolute
and must be preserved in all features, agreements, and communications.
