# Sector Overview — AU QSR (Daniels Donuts Lens)

## Scope

This skill produces a targeted overview of the Australian QSR / specialty donut sector, grounded in the LK Group knowledge base. It is not a generic market report — every section should be framed around what it means for [[Daniels Donuts]].

## Market Anchors (pre-loaded — cite these, don't re-research)

- AU QSR market: USD 5.5B (2025), CAGR 3.72% to 2034
- AU donut market: AUD ~$329M (2025), CAGR 4.80% to 2035
- QSR = 43.2% of total AU foodservice
- 2025: net +250 store openings — strongest growth on record
- Labour ~40% of QSR operating costs; wage growth outpacing productivity
