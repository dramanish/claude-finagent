# Collins Foods

**ASX: CKF**
**Format:** KFC Australia operator + KFC Germany + (exiting) Taco Bell AU
**Role in digest:** QSR consumer health bellwether — not a direct competitor, but the best public signal for how value-oriented AU consumers are spending

## FY25 Results
- Revenue: $1,519.5M
- Underlying NPAT: $137.9M
- Full-year dividend: 26.0 cents per share (fully franked)
- KFC AU same-store sales: H2 +0.6% (improved from H1 -0.1%)
- Digital = 34.2% of sales (delivery, app, kiosk, click & collect)

## Strategic Direction
- Exiting Taco Bell Australia — licence transfer in progress
- Accelerating Germany: committed to 40–70 new KFC outlets over 5 years
- Laser focus on KFC AU: SSS growth, margin, and service

## Why It Matters for [[Daniels Donuts]]
Collins Foods is the best listed proxy for value-consumer QSR health in Australia. When KFC AU SSS softens, it signals discretionary pressure on the same customer base Daniels serves. Monitor every half-year result as a leading indicator.

## Watch For
- KFC AU same-store sales each reporting period
- Any commentary on consumer spending behaviour or trade-down
- Digital adoption trajectory (benchmark for the category)
