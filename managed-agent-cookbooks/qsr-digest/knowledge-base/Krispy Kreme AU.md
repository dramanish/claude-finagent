# Krispy Kreme AU

**Ownership:** Fully owned by Krispy Kreme Inc. (US-listed)
**Format:** Doughnut shops + petrol/convenience + QSR partnerships
**Threat level to [[Daniels Donuts]]: HIGH — channel expansion compresses Daniels' convenience advantage**

## Recent Moves (2025)
- **Hungry Jack's partnership** — doughnuts at 56 Sydney Hungry Jack's from May 2025 (trial ran Nov 2024 in Wollongong, Newcastle, ACT)
- **Ampol partnership** — fresh doughnuts at 30 Foodary locations across NSW and Melbourne from June 2025
- **Townsville drive-through** — new store May 2025 with high-capacity production line
- CEO flagging intent to find master franchisee partners for Asia-Pacific expansion

## Why It Matters
Channel proliferation is the key threat. Krispy Kreme doesn't need more stores — it's embedding into existing high-traffic retail (fast food, petrol). Every new distribution point is an impulse purchase moment that Daniels doesn't have.

## Watch For
- Further QSR or convenience retail partnerships
- Expansion beyond Sydney for the Hungry Jack's rollout
- Master franchisee announcements for Asia-Pacific
