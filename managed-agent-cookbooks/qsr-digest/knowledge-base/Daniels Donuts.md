# Daniels Donuts

Core [[LK Group]] QSR portfolio company. Founded 2016 in Springvale VIC by Daniel Bartalotta. [[Queens Lane Capital]] acquired controlling stake August 2021.

## Scale (mid-2025)
- 50+ locations across VIC and NSW
- 10 stores by 2019 → 25 VIC stores Sep 2022 → 40th store (Southern Cross Station) Jul 2023 → 50+ by 2025
- Sydney expansion actively underway

## Product
- 50+ donut flavours (incl. gluten-free, vegan)
- Barista coffee, pies, milkshakes
- Food truck (F1 GP 2023, events)

## Positioning
Value-oriented specialty donuts — accessible price point, generous portions, 24/7 stores. Cult following in Melbourne's south-east suburbs. Not premium/artisan; competes on value, variety, and volume.

- vs [[Krispy Kreme AU]]: different brand personality, more local/community feel
- vs [[Donut King]]: fresher, better experience, less corporate
- vs [[Brooklyn Donuts]]: overlapping specialty gap, competing for same "not Donut King" customer

## Strategic Priorities
- Accelerate NSW footprint
- Drive digital and loyalty adoption (currently behind [[Donut King]] on digital)
- Build occasions/catering channel (high ATV, low marginal cost)
- Manage labour cost pressure at scale — see [[AU QSR Sector]]
- Evaluate new store formats (drive-through, kiosk)
