# Brooklyn Donuts

**Ownership:** Private, founder-operated
**HQ:** Queensland
**Format:** Specialty donut + coffee, franchise model
**Threat level to [[Daniels Donuts]]: MEDIUM — competing for the same "not Donut King" positioning**

## Scale (2025)
- 20 stores by April 2025
- Present in QLD, NSW, ACT
- First NSW store opened Oct 2023; national footprint ambition stated publicly
- Franchise model — actively marketing new territories

## Positioning
Premium/specialty donuts. International aesthetic (New York-inspired). Sits in the same gap as Daniels — not corporate franchise (Donut King), not US chain ([[Krispy Kreme AU]]). Competes on brand and experience.

## Why It Matters
Brooklyn Donuts is the most direct positioning competitor to [[Daniels Donuts]] in the specialty segment. If Brooklyn opens in VIC, that's a direct trade area threat. Their franchise expansion pace is a signal of how much institutional capital is backing this format.

## Watch For
- Any VIC store openings
- Franchise recruitment advertising (signals expansion capital)
- Any investment/PE backing announcements
- Store count milestones
