# LK Group

Melbourne-based private investment and operating group. Invests in Australian businesses, then actively operates and scales them post-acquisition.

## Arms
- [[Queens Lane Capital]] — private equity arm
- Direct operating management of portfolio businesses

## Portfolio (selected)
- [[Daniels Donuts]] — QSR, acquired Aug 2021
- Petra Industries — building materials (Signorino, Multiform, Woodcut)
- Brand Collective / PAS Group — fashion retail
- Hypnos Group — sleep & bedding (Snooze, Future Sleep, G&G Furniture)
- FUZE Technology — $11.5M Series A, Jun 2024

## Investment Philosophy
Back founder-led or brand-led businesses with loyal customer base and clear room to scale through capital, operational improvement, and M&A.
