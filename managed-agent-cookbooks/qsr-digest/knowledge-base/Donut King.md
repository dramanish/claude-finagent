# Donut King

**Parent:** Retail Food Group (ASX: RFG)
**Scale:** ~203 outlets nationally
**Threat level to [[Daniels Donuts]]: MEDIUM — digital maturity and distribution coverage are ahead**

## Recent Moves (2024–2025)
- **Donut King Occasions** — occasion-based online ordering (birthdays, corporate, baby showers). ATV 3.8× higher than in-store. Full Queensland rollout June 2024.
- **57% digital sales growth** FY25 — strongest digital momentum in the donut category
- RFG strategic reset: selling corporately-operated Donut King stores to franchisees; reinvesting capital into Beefy's Pies and Firehouse Subs
- RFG swung to $15M loss FY25 as group sales flatlined — but Donut King was a **standout performer**
- International: new Turkey roasting/supply hub to support master franchise expansion

## RFG Context
RFG is restructuring — offloading low-margin corporate stores and doubling down on franchise model. Donut King is one of the brands they're keeping and investing in. Financially stressed parent but the Donut King brand itself is performing.

## Why It Matters
Donut King's 57% digital growth is a capability benchmark [[Daniels Donuts]] needs to close. The Occasions channel (3.8× ATV) is exactly the type of high-margin, low-footprint revenue that scales well.

## Watch For
- RFG half-year and full-year results (Donut King segment performance)
- Occasions channel rollout beyond QLD
- Any change to RFG's ownership structure or recapitalisation
