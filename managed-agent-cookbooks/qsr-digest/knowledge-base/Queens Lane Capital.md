# Queens Lane Capital

Private equity arm of [[LK Group]]. Focuses on Australian mid-market businesses.

## Strategy
- Enterprise value: sub-$100M
- Deal types: leveraged buyouts, growth equity, consolidations, carve-outs, turnarounds
- Backs businesses with strong brand or customer loyalty where capital + operational improvement unlocks growth

## Key Investment — QSR
- [[Daniels Donuts]] — controlling stake acquired August 2021
- Funded rapid expansion from ~10 stores to 50+ across VIC and NSW
